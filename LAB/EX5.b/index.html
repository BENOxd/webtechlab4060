Welcome to the Valorant Fan Website

Explore guides, tips, and updates for Valorant enthusiasts!

Valorant is a tactical first-person shooter developed by Riot Games, released on June 2, 2020. It features 5v5 matches where teams alternate between attacking and defending, with the attacking team aiming to plant a "Spike" (bomb) and the defenders trying to stop them or defuse the Spike. The game combines precise gunplay with unique abilities for each "Agent," adding strategic depth to matches. With multiple game modes like Competitive, Unrated, Spike Rush, and Deathmatch, Valorant emphasizes teamwork, strategy, and skill. Its esports scene has grown significantly, making it a favorite among competitive gamers worldwide.
